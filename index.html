About Me

Emerald Green, Consumer Assistance Program Coordinator at CCHI, serves as the Program Coordinator (20% FTE) for the F2F project at FVCO. Originally from Memphis, Tennessee, Ms. Green relocated to Colorado for her undergraduate studies at Colorado College and completed B.A. degrees in Organismal Biology and Ecology and Classics. She is also a certified EMT and has assisted in therapy and care for college athletes. Ms. Green has worked for Food to Power in community outreach and engagement. Committed to advancing health equity, Ms. Green has completed consumer assistance training at CCHI to assist privately, publicly, and uninsured members in medical billing and claims issues. She will be responsible for F2F outreach and provide individual assistance to families, as well as assisting in FVCO's legislative initiatives.

Now, she is a student of the DU Coding Bootcamp and is interested in establishing a full time career in technological engineering.
